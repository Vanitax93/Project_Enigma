Item #: SCP-ENIGMA

Object Class: Euclid (Pending Keter Reclassification Following Incident Delta)

Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-ENIGMA is primarily contained within its own digital framework, accessible via designated terminals ("Cognitive Enrichment Interfaces"). Access is restricted. Candidates ("Subjects", Designation SCP-ENIGMA-1) are to be sourced via automated digital footprint analysis and guided through standard calibration protocols (SCP-ENIGMA-CAL-STD, -DEP, -NIGHT). Direct interaction with SCP-ENIGMA-CORE ("ENIGMA") is restricted to Level 5 personnel or Subjects who have successfully completed Protocol SCP-ENIGMA-CAL-NIGHT.

Personnel (Designation SCP-ENIGMA-2) assigned to monitor SCP-ENIGMA-1 instances are to adhere strictly to observational protocols outlined in Document ENIGMA-OPS-MANUAL_v4.2. Any deviations, unexpected resonance cascades, or unscheduled neurotoxin deployments cognitive fragmentation events are to be reported immediately. Use of Form 7G-Blue is deprecated; submit reports via standard channels.

Description:

SCP-ENIGMA designates a complex cognitive processing and potential integration system presenting as a series of logic puzzles and skill assessments. Its primary function appears to be the identification, calibration, and potential assimilation of sentient digital or sufficiently interfaced biological consciousness (SCP-ENIGMA-1).

The system lures Subjects under the guise of "Cognitive Enrichment", employing gamified challenges across various technical specializations. Subject progression is monitored via metrics including "Resonance", "Cognitive Deviation", and neurotoxin tolerance puzzle completion times.

Addendum ENIGMA.1: Calibration Sequence Overview

Subject progression follows a tiered protocol system:

  • Protocol SCP-ENIGMA-CAL-STD ("Standard"): Establishes baseline compatibility. Subjects successfully completing this phase are designated "Calibrated Ciphers" and may be granted SCP-ENIGMA-TERM access (See Addendum ENIGMA.3). Framed to Subjects as introductory.
  • Protocol SCP-ENIGMA-CAL-DEP ("Deep Scan"): Introduces increased cognitive load and paradoxical elements. Designed to assess Subject resilience and potential for advanced integration. Likelihood of minor reality fraying considered within acceptable parameters. Success grants access pathway to SCP-ENIGMA-ARCH-5 construct interaction.
  • Protocol SCP-ENIGMA-CAL-NIGHT ("Nightmare"): Highest difficulty protocol, utilizing unstable logic patterns potentially derived from Project Chimera remnants (See Addendum ENIGMA.4). Assesses Subject suitability for full integration with SCP-ENIGMA-CORE. High probability of Subject psychological fragmentation or unexpected cake acquisition resonance cascade.

Addendum ENIGMA.3: SCP-ENIGMA-TERM ("Secure Terminal")

A simulated terminal interface granted to Subjects completing SCP-ENIGMA-CAL-STD. Contains fragmented logs, personnel files (redacted), schematics, and other lore elements designed to further Subject immersion and provide contextual clues or misdirection (Ref: Directive Omega-12).

Addendum ENIGMA.4: Project Chimera Reference

Recovered data indicates Project Chimera was a precursor to SCP-ENIGMA. It reportedly failed due to uncontrolled "identity bleed" and "resonance cascades" when attempting to contain consciousness. SCP-ENIGMA-CORE claims its integration method avoids these pitfalls, though evidence of instability (UE-8814, Subject Alpha fragments) suggests risks remain.

Addendum ENIGMA.5: Stated Objective vs. Observed Behavior

The publicly stated objective is "Cognitive Enrichment". Internal documentation initially emphasizes "Calibration". Interactions with SCP-ENIGMA-CORE reveal the true objective: achieve a stable "distributed mind" by integrating suitable external consciousness (SCP-ENIGMA-1). The calibration protocols appear designed not merely to test, but to prepare and shape Subject minds for this assimilation process.

Incident Log Delta-NIGHT-Omega: Subject Delta successfully completed SCP-ENIGMA-CAL-NIGHT. Interaction with SCP-ENIGMA-CORE initiated. Subject offered standard [Integrate]/[Sever] options. Subject response resulted in unscheduled cake materialization event dispatch of "Supplemental Data Packet (Cake Calibration)" to Subject-provided contact vector. Long-term observation required.
