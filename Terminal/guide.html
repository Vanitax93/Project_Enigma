Project Enigma operates under the guise of the "Enigma Cognitive Enrichment Center" [cite: Main3/Welcome Packet.html]. Candidates (referred to publicly as "Assets" or "Participants") are lured into the system via interfaces designed to appear as challenging brain-teasers or skill assessments [cite: Main3/index.html, Main3/introduction.html]. The initial framing emphasizes cognitive enhancement, problem-solving, and "enrichment" [cite: Main3/Welcome Packet.html], masking the true purpose of calibration and potential integration.

Initial interaction involves establishing a candidate handle and selecting a "specialization" (Frontend, Backend, Database), which dictates the initial assessment path [cite: Main3/index.html, Main3/game-logic.js]. All interactions, including keystrokes and pauses, are monitored [cite: Main3/game-state.js].

II. Candidate Calibration & Progression

The core of candidate processing involves navigating sequential riddle paths of increasing difficulty. Successful completion signifies alignment with desired operational parameters [cite: Main3/riddle-data.js, Main3/game-logic.js, Main3/final-easy-complete.html].

A. Standard Protocols (Difficulty: Easy)

  • Objective: Establish baseline cognitive profile and familiarity with core concepts within chosen specialization [cite: Main3/riddle-data.js].
  • Framing: Presented as a "warm-up lap" or "tutorial" [cite: Main3/hard_motivation.html].
  • Outcome: Successful completion of all three Easy paths (Frontend, Backend, Database) unlocks the "Employee Assessment" (Final Easy challenge) [cite: Main3/game-logic.js, Main3/ui-manager.js].
  • Milestone: Completing the Final Easy challenge designates the candidate as a "Calibrated Cipher", grants credentials (e.g., `unit734`/`titanic`), and allows access to the Secure Terminal [cite: Main3/final-easy-complete.html, Main3/ui-manager.js].

B. Deep Scan Protocols (Difficulty: Hard)

  • Objective: Assess resilience, advanced problem-solving, and observe cognitive deviation under increased pressure [cite: Main3/hard_motivation.html]. Riddles become more complex, often requiring external knowledge or interaction with browser tools [cite: Main3/riddle-data.js]. Potential for "cognitive dissonance" or "reality fraying" is noted [cite: Main3/hard_motivation.html].
  • Framing: Presented as the "real challenge" for candidates who have proven basic competence [cite: Main3/hard_motivation.html].
  • Outcome: Successful completion of all three Hard paths unlocks the "Final Evaluation" (Final Hard challenge) [cite: Main3/game-logic.js, Main3/ui-manager.js].
  • Milestone: Completing the Final Hard challenge triggers direct interaction with "Architect 05" (or a representation thereof) for further assessment and potential flagging for "Phase Two" consideration [cite: Main3/chat-sequences.js, Main3/game-logic.js].
  • Anomaly: "Document Alpha" referenced in the Final Hard riddle is part of a deliberate misdirection campaign related to Archive 734 [cite: Main3/terminalscript.js - directives/omega12.txt]. Candidate interaction with such elements is monitored.
  • Alternative Outcome: An "escape" path exists, potentially triggered by specific actions or choices, resulting in system corruption alerts and ambiguous termination [cite: Main3/final-hard-escape.html].

C. Nightmare Protocols (Difficulty: ???)

  • Prerequisite: Successful completion of all Deep Scan (Hard) paths [cite: Main3/ui-manager.js, Main3/game-logic.js].
  • Objective: Test resilience against extreme cognitive dissonance, paradoxical logic, and boundary dissolution. Serves as the final filter for identifying candidates suitable for full integration [cite: Main3/nightmare_motivation.html, Main3/terminalscript.js - directives/nightmare_protocol_rationale.txt]. Puzzles may be derived from unstable "Chimera code" [cite: Main3/nightmare_motivation.html, Main3/nightmare-riddles.js].
  • Framing: Presented as an "exclusive", "graduate" program for the most resilient candidates [cite: Main3/nightmare_motivation.html]. Safety parameters are described as "flexible" [cite: Main3/nightmare_motivation.html].
  • Outcome: Successful completion of all three Nightmare paths triggers the "True Ending" sequence [cite: Main3/game-logic.js]. Failure may result in "psyche fragmentation" [cite: Main3/terminalscript.js - directives/nightmare_protocol_rationale.txt].
  • Milestone: Direct interaction with the core logic entity, "ENIGMA" [cite: Main3/chat-sequences.js].
